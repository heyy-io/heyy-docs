## Workspace Configuration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Workspace Name">
    The workspace name identifies your organization within Heyy. This name may be visible in certain integrations and reports.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Edit Workspace Name">
        Enter your desired workspace name in the text field
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save Changes">
        Click **Save changes** to update
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Timezone">
    Set your workspace timezone to ensure accurate scheduling and time-based features.

    <Note>
      The timezone affects:

      - Working hours display
      - Scheduled message delivery
      - Analytics and reports timestamps
      - Automated workflow timing
    </Note>
    Select your timezone from the dropdown menu and save changes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Danger Zone

<Warning>
  **Delete Workspace** - This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your workspace and all associated data cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

To delete your workspace:

1. Click **Permanently delete workspace.**
2. Confirm the deletion.
3. All data, conversations, and settings will be permanently removed.
