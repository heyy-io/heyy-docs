Create custom views to filter and organize your pages.

<Frame>
  ![Views Settings](/images/settings-views.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  Go to [**Settings \> Views**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/views)
</Info>

## Creating Views

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create View">
    Click **\+ Create view** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your View">
    Enter a descriptive name
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Filters">
    Configure filter criteria to determine which items appear in the view
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save your custom view
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Create views to quickly access specific conversation types, such as "Unassigned", "High Priority", or "Awaiting Response".
</Tip>
