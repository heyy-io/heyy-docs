# Settings Overview

The Settings page allows you to customize and configure your Heyy workspace to match your business needs. Access Settings by clicking the gear icon located on the top right or clicking **Settings** from the profile dropdown menu on the top right.

## Profile

Manage your personal profile settings and preferences to personalize your Heyy experience.

<Frame>
  ![Profile Settings](/images/settings-profile.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  Go to [**Settings \> Profile**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/profile)
</Info>

### Available Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Full Name">
    Update your first and last name that will be displayed across the platform and in communications with your team.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Edit Name Fields">
        Update the **First name** and **Last name** fields
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save Changes">
        Click **Save changes** to apply your updates
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Email Address">
    Your email address is used for login and notifications. To update your email:

    1. Click the **Update** button next to the email field
    2. Enter your new email address
    3. Verify the new email through the confirmation link sent to your inbox

    <Warning>
      Changing your email will require re-verification and may affect your login credentials.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Phone Number">
    Add or update your phone number for account recovery and optional SMS notifications.

    1. Click the **Update** button next to the phone number field
    2. Select your country code
    3. Enter your phone number
    4. Verify via SMS code if required
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Message Notifications">
    Toggle message notifications on or off to control whether you receive alerts for new messages.

    - **Active**: You will receive notifications for new messages
    - **Inactive**: Notifications are silenced

    <Tip>
      Enable notifications to stay responsive to customer inquiries in real-time.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Working Hours

Configure your business working hours to let customers know when you're available and to manage automated responses.

<Frame>
  ![Working Hours Settings](/images/settings-working-hours.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  Go to [**Settings \> Working Hours**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/working-hours)
</Info>

### Weekly Hours

Set up your regular business hours for each day of the week.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable/Disable Days">
    Toggle the switch next to each day to mark it as available or unavailable
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Hours">
    For enabled days, set the start and end times using the time pickers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Multiple Shifts">
    Click **\+ Add** to add multiple time ranges for a single day (e.g., split shifts)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Configuration">
    Click **Save changes** to apply your working hours
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Date Specific Hours

Set special hours for holidays, events, or any specific dates that differ from your regular schedule.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Special Date">
    Click **\+ Add date specific hours**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Date">
    Choose the specific date from the calendar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Hours">
    Set the working hours for that date, or dont select hours to make this day unavailable
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Click Apply
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Date-specific hours override weekly hours for the specified dates. Use this for holidays, special events, or temporary schedule changes.
</Note>

## Billing

View and manage your subscription, credits, and wallet for paid features.

### Subscription Overview

Your current plan and billing status are displayed at the top:

- **Plan Name** (e.g., Enterprise)
- **Monthly Cost**
- **Manage Subscription** - Access your subscription portal
- **Upgrade Plan** - View available plan upgrades

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Credits">
    Credits are consumed by AI Employee messages and workflow triggers.

    <Frame>
      ![Credits Tab](/images/settings-billing-credits.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Credits**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/credits)
    </Info>
    **Credit Features:**

    - View total available credits
    - Track credits used this billing cycle
    - See when credits reset

    **Add Additional Credits:** Click **\+ Add additional credits** to purchase more credits at \$12 per 1,000 credits.

    **Auto Recharge Credits:** Enable automatic credit purchases when your balance falls below a threshold at \$14 per 1,000 credits.

    <Tip>
      Enable Auto Recharge to ensure your AI Employees and workflows never stop due to insufficient credits.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Wallet">
    Your wallet balance is used for paid channel costs (e.g., WhatsApp message fees).

    <Frame>
      ![Wallet Tab](/images/settings-billing-wallet.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Wallet**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/credits)
    </Info>
    **Wallet Features:**

    - View current wallet balance in USD
    - Add funds to your wallet
    - Configure Auto Recharge

    **Auto Recharge Wallet:** Enable automatic wallet top-ups when your balance falls below a certain threshold.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Invoices">
    View and download your billing history and paid invoices.

    <Frame>
      ![Invoices Tab](/images/settings-billing-invoices.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Invoices**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/invoices)
    </Info>
    The invoice table shows:

    - **Number** - Invoice reference number
    - **Date** - Invoice date
    - **Amount** - Total charged
    - **Status** - Payment status (Paid, Pending, etc.)

    Click on any invoice to download or view details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## WhatsApp Templates

Create and manage WhatsApp message templates for outbound communications.

<Frame>
  ![WhatsApp Templates Settings](/images/settings-whatsapp-templates.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  Go to [**Settings \> WhatsApp Message Templates**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/whatsapp-templates)
</Info>

### Template Overview

WhatsApp requires pre-approved templates for initiating conversations. Your templates table shows:

- **Name** - Template identifier
- **Channel** - Associated WhatsApp channel
- **Status** - Approval status (Active, Pending, Rejected)
- **Category** - Template type (Marketing, Utility, Authentication)
- **Created At** - Creation date

### Creating Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Template">
    Click **\+ New Template** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Template">
    - Enter a template name
    - Select the category
    - Choose the language
    - Write your message content with placeholders
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Components">
    Optionally add:

    - Header (text, image, video, document)
    - Body with variables
    - Footer
    - Quick reply or call-to-action buttons
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for Approval">
    Submit the template to WhatsApp for review
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Templates must comply with WhatsApp's policies. Marketing templates require opt-in consent from recipients.
</Warning>

### Template Categories

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Marketing">
    Promotional messages, offers, and announcements. Requires explicit opt-in from recipients.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Utility">
    Transactional messages like order updates, appointment reminders, and account notifications.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Authentication">
    One-time passwords and verification codes.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Webhooks

Configure webhooks to receive real-time notifications when events occur in your Heyy workspace.

<Frame>
  ![Webhooks Settings](/images/settings-webhooks.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  Go to [**Settings \> Webhooks**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/api-webhooks)
</Info>

### Creating Webhooks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create Webhook">
    Click **\+ Create webhook** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Endpoint URL">
    Provide the URL where webhook events should be sent
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Events">
    Choose which events should trigger the webhook:

    - New message received
    - Conversation assigned
    - Contact created/updated
    - And more...
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save your webhook configuration
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Webhook Events

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Message Events">
    - `message.received` - New inbound message
    - `message.sent` - Outbound message delivered
    - `message.failed` - Message delivery failed
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Conversation Events">
    - `conversation.created` - New conversation started
    - `conversation.assigned` - Conversation assigned to agent
    - `conversation.resolved` - Conversation marked as resolved
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Contact Events">
    - `contact.created` - New contact added
    - `contact.updated` - Contact information changed
    - `contact.deleted` - Contact removed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Use webhooks to:

  - Sync contacts with your CRM
  - Trigger workflows in other tools
  - Log conversations for analytics
  - Send notifications to your team
</Tip>
