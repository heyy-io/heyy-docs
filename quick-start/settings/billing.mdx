## Subscription Overview

Your current plan and billing status are displayed at the top:

- **Plan Name** (e.g., Enterprise)
- **Monthly Cost**
- **Manage Subscription** - Access your subscription portal
- **Upgrade Plan** - View available plan upgrades

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Credits">
    Credits are consumed by AI Employee messages and workflow triggers.

    <Frame>
      ![Credits Tab](/assets/settings-billing-credits.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Credits**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/credits)
    </Info>
    **Credit Features:**

    - View total available credits
    - Track credits used this billing cycle
    - See when credits reset

    **Add Additional Credits:** Click **\+ Add additional credits** to purchase more credits at \$12 per 1,000 credits.

    **Auto Recharge Credits:** Enable automatic credit purchases when your balance falls below a threshold at \$14 per 1,000 credits.

    <Tip>
      Enable Auto Recharge to ensure your AI Employees and workflows never stop due to insufficient credits.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Wallet">
    Your wallet balance is used for paid channel costs (e.g., WhatsApp message fees).

    <Frame>
      ![Wallet Tab](/images/settings-billing-wallet.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Wallet**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/credits)
    </Info>
    **Wallet Features:**

    - View current wallet balance in USD
    - Add funds to your wallet
    - Configure Auto Recharge

    **Auto Recharge Wallet:** Enable automatic wallet top-ups when your balance falls below a certain threshold.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Invoices">
    View and download your billing history and paid invoices.

    <Frame>
      ![Invoices Tab](/images/settings-billing-invoices.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Invoices**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/invoices)
    </Info>
    The invoice table shows:

    - **Number** - Invoice reference number
    - **Date** - Invoice date
    - **Amount** - Total charged
    - **Status** - Payment status (Paid, Pending, etc.)

    Click on any invoice to download or view details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
