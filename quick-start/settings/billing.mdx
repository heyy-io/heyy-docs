---
title: "Billing"
description: "View and manage your subscription, credits, and wallet for paid features."
---

<Info>
  Go to [**Settings \> Billing**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing) to manage your subscription.
</Info>

Your current plan and billing status are displayed at the top:

- **Plan Name** (e.g., Pro)
- **Monthly Cost**
- **Manage Subscription** - Access your subscription portal, to update billing details and manage payment methods.
- **Upgrade Plan** - View available plan upgrades.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Credits">
    With each one of our plans you will get montlhy credits that reset with the plan every month (for yearly plans the credits will still reset monthly). Credits are consumed by AI Employee (1 credit per AI answer) and automations (1 credit per automation trigger).

    <Frame>
      ![Credits Tab](/assets/settings-billing-credits.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Credits**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/credits) to manage your credits.
    </Info>
    **Credit Features:**

    - View total available credits.
    - Track credits used this billing cycle.
    - See when credits reset date.

    **Auto Recharge Credits:** Enable automatic credit purchases when your balance falls below a threshold, at \$14 per 1,000 credits.

    <Tip>
      Enable Auto Recharge to ensure your AI Employees and automations never stop due to insufficient credits.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Wallet">
    Your wallet balance is used for paid channel costs (e.g., WhatsApp message fees).

    <Frame>
      ![Wallet Tab](/assets/settings-billing-wallet.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Wallet**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/credits) to manage your wallet.
    </Info>
    **Wallet Features:**

    - View current wallet balance in USD
    - Add funds to your wallet
    - Configure Auto Recharge

    **Auto Recharge Wallet:** Enable automatic wallet top-ups when your balance falls below a certain threshold.

    <Tip>
      Enable Auto Recharge to ensure your WhatsApp message templates won't fail due to insufficient funds.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Invoices">
    View and download your billing history and paid invoices.

    <Frame>
      ![Invoices Tab](/assets/settings-billing-invoices.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Invoices**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/invoices) to manage your invoices
    </Info>
    The invoice table shows:

    - **Number** - Invoice reference number
    - **Date** - Invoice date
    - **Amount** - Total charged
    - **Status** - Payment status (Paid, Pending, etc.)

    Click on any invoice to download or view details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
