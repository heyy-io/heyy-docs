---

<Note>
  Go to [**Settings \> Billing**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing) to manage your subscription.
</Note>

Your current plan and plan actions are displayed at the top:

- **Plan Name** & **Monthly Cost**
- **Manage Subscription** - Access your subscription portal to update billing details and manage payment methods.
- **Upgrade Plan** - View available plan upgrades.

## Credits

Each plan includes monthly credits that reset every month. Credits are consumed by AI Employee and automations [see pricing](https://assets.heyy.io/website/credit-pricing.pdf). In the Credits section, you can:

- **View balance** - See your current credit balance and usage
- **Manage Auto Recharge** - Configure automatic credit purchases when your balance falls below a threshold. See [Auto Recharge Credits](#auto-recharge-credits) for more details

<Frame>
  ![Credits Tab](/assets/settings-billing-credits.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Credits**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/credits) to manage your credits.
</Note>

### Auto Recharge Credits

Enable automatic credit purchases when your balance falls below a threshold.

<Tip>
  Enable Auto Recharge to ensure your AI Employees and automations never stop due to insufficient credits.
</Tip>

## Wallet

Your wallet balance is used for paid channel costs (e.g., WhatsApp template message).
For full WhatsApp pricing details, see the [WhatsApp pricing table](https://assets.heyy.io/website/whatsapp-pricing.pdf).
In the Wallet section, you can:

- **Add funds** - Click **Add to wallet** to immediately add funds to your wallet for use
- **View balance** - See your current wallet balance displayed in USD
- **Manage Auto Recharge** - Configure automatic wallet top-ups when your balance falls below a threshold. See [Auto Recharge Wallet](#auto-recharge-wallet) for more details

<Frame>
  ![Wallet Tab](/assets/settings-billing-wallet.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Wallet**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/wallet) to manage your wallet.
</Note>

### Auto Recharge Wallet

Enable automatic wallet top-ups when your balance falls below a certain threshold.

<Tip>
  Enable Auto Recharge to ensure your WhatsApp message templates won't fail due to insufficient funds.
</Tip>

## Invoices

View and download your billing history and invoices.

<Frame>
  ![Invoices Tab](/assets/settings-billing-invoices.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Go to [**Settings \> Billing \> Invoices**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/billing/invoices) to manage your invoices.
</Note>

The invoice table displays:

- **Number** - Invoice reference number
- **Date** - Invoice date
- **Amount** - Total amount charged
- **Status** - Payment status (Paid, Pending, etc.)

Click any invoice to download or view its details.
