# Channels

Manage your communication channels and connect new ones.

<Info>
  Go to [**Settings \> Channels**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/channels)
</Info>

<Frame>
  ![Channels Settings](/images/settings-channels.png)
</Frame>

## Channel Overview

Each connected channel displays:

- **Channel Icon** (WhatsApp, etc.)
- **Channel Name** - Your custom name for the channel
- **Phone Number** - Associated phone number
- **Status** - Active or Inactive

## Managing Channels

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add New Channel">
    Click **\+ Add channel** to connect a new communication channel
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Channel">
    Follow the setup wizard for your chosen channel type
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Channel">
    Click the edit icon to modify channel settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Channel Actions">
    Use the action menu for additional options like disconnecting
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Currently supported channels include WhatsApp Business API. More channels may be available depending on your plan.
</Note>
