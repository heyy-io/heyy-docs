## Adding Channel

To add a channel click on the **Add channel** button and then select the channel type you want to connect:

- [Live Chat](/channels/live-chat)

- [WhatsApp](/channels/whatsapp)

- [Instagram](/channels/instagram)

- [Facebook Messenger](/channels/facebook)
