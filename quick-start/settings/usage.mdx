<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Credits Usage">
    Track how your credits are being consumed over time.

    <Frame>
      ![Credits Usage](/assets/settings-usage-credits.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Usage \> Credits**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/usage/credits) to view your usage.
    </Info>
    **Metrics Displayed:**

    - **Total Usage** - Credits consumed in selected period
    - **AI Employee Messages** - Credits used by AI responses
    - **Workflow Triggers** - Credits used by automation workflows

    **Date Range Selection:** Use the date picker to view usage for specific time periods.

    <Tip>
      Analyze your usage patterns to optimize AI Employee configurations and workflow triggers.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Wallet Usage">
    Monitor your wallet spending on paid channels.

    <Frame>
      ![Wallet Usage](/assets/settings-usage-wallet.png)
    </Frame>
    <Info>
      Go to [**Settings \> Usage \> Wallet**](https://app.heyy.io/settings/usage/wallet)
    </Info>
    **Metrics Displayed:**

    - **Total Usage** - Amount spent in selected period
    - **WhatsApp Utility Template Messages** - Cost for utility messages
    - **WhatsApp Marketing Template Messages** - Cost for marketing messages

    The chart visualizes daily spending trends.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
