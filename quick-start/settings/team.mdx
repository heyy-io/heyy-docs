## Team Management

Your team page displays all members with their:

- **Name** - Team member's full name
- **Email** - Login email address
- **Role** - Permission level (Admin, Member, etc.)

## Inviting Team Members

<Frame>
  ![Date Specific Hours](/assets/settings-team-example-1.gif)
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Invite Member">
    Click the **\+ Invite member** button in the top right
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Provide the team member's email addresses
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    The invited member will receive an email to join your workspace
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Existing Members

Use the action menu (three dots) next to each team member to:

- Edit their role
- Remove them from the workspace
