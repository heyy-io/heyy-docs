The **Settings** feature is the engine room of your business operations on Heyy. It defines how everything works, laying the **core foundation for your customer messaging journey** and overall platform usage.

## Accessing Settings

To access **Settings**:

1. Go to the top right corner of your Heyy Dashboard (Heyy Hub).
2. Click the **gear icon** next to your username.
3. You will land on the **Settings page**, where all configuration options are available in the left panel.

---

## General Settings

The **General** settings group contains the foundational options that define your business and team setup.

### Profile

Manage your personal information and notification preferences:

- **Full name** – update first and last name  
- **Email address** – view and edit  
- **Phone number** – view and edit  
- **Message notification sounds** – enable or disable  

### Business

Configure your business-specific settings:

- **Business name** – displayed across the platform  
- **Time zone** – ensures accurate scheduling and timestamps  
- **Logo (optional)** – used throughout Heyy  
- **Working hours** – set the business hours for each day of the week; reflects across all channels  

### Members

Manage your team:

- Add new members to your account  
- View existing members and their information  

### Teams

Organize your team into roles or groups:

- Create teams for specific business functions  
- Assign members to relevant teams  

### Views

Create custom **views** for your contacts:

- Organize contacts into different views for easier navigation  
- Useful for businesses with large contact lists  
- Quickly access subsets of your contacts without browsing the entire database  

---

## Channel Settings

The **Channel** settings group allows you to manage how your business interacts with customers.

### Channels

Configure the different messaging channels connected to your business (e.g., WhatsApp, Messenger, etc.).

### WhatsApp Templates

Create new **WhatsApp templates** for your business communications:

- Predefined messages for notifications, promotions, or updates  

### Quick Replies

Set up **quick replies** to respond faster to common customer inquiries  

---

## Contacts Settings

The **Contacts** settings group allows you to organize and classify your contacts:

- **Labels** – categorize contacts based on attributes, interests, or behavior  
- **Attributes** – add custom properties to contacts for better segmentation and personalization  

---

## Developer Settings

The **Developers** settings group provides tools for integrations and technical configurations.

### API Keys

Create and manage **API keys** for integrating Heyy with other platforms  

### Webhooks

Create **webhooks** to receive real-time notifications from Heyy  

### Documentation

Access official Heyy **documentation** for developers, APIs, and integrations
