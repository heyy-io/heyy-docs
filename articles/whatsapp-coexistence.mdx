## IV. Number Portability Management: Disconnecting from a Previous Provider

If the business is currently using its number with the WhatsApp Business Platform through a third-party Business Solution Provider (BSP), a mandatory clean disconnection process must be performed through the Meta Business Suite before onboarding to Heyy using Coexistence.

### 4.1. The Critical Requirement for Disconnection

A phone number can only be bound to one WABA profile and one solution provider simultaneously. If the number is still actively linked to a legacy BSP, Heyyâ€™s onboarding will fail, citing an "in use" or "not eligible" error. The business must cleanly delete the number from the previous WABA profile before attempting CoExistence setup with Heyy.

### 4.2. Mandatory Pre-Deletion Sequence

To avoid the profile becoming administratively "stuck" or retaining phantom permissions, a crucial sequence dependency must be observed:

1. **Delete Profile from Old Platform:** The customer should first log into the previous provider's platform and actively **delete the WhatsApp profile from that legacy account**.
2. **Delete Number in Meta Manager:** Only after removing the profile from the previous platform should the number deletion proceed in the Meta Business Suite. This sequence is designed to ensure a clean administrative break.

### 4.3. Step-by-Step: Deleting the Phone Number via Meta Business Suite (Primary Method)

The deletion process is executed entirely within Meta's environment, removing the phone number from the previous WABA profile:

1. **Access Meta Business Suite:** Log into the Facebook account that holds administrative rights over the WABA.
2. **Navigate to WhatsApp Accounts:** Go to **Settings** on the bottom left. In the menu, locate the **Accounts** section, and click **WhatsApp accounts**.
3. **Access WhatsApp Manager:** Select the specific WhatsApp Business account associated with the previous provider, and click **WhatsApp Manager**.
4. **Locate and Delete the Number:** Within the WhatsApp Manager, find the phone number to be ported/deleted. Click the **trash can icon**.
5. **Confirm Deletion:** Follow the final instructions in the dialog and **confirm the deletion**.
6. **Post-Deletion Wait Time:** It is recommended to allow the number to be released fully by the system. The number may take up to **3 minutes** to become available for re-registration with Heyy.

### 4.4. Disconnection Troubleshooting: Revoking Partner Permissions (Secondary Method)

If the primary deletion method is insufficient or the number remains blocked, the administrative link to the previous solution provider may need manual revocation:

1. **Access Partners Panel:** Sign into Meta Business Suite. Navigate to the **Settings** (gear icon) -\> **Users** -\> **Partners** panel.
2. **Manage Assets:** Locate the name of the previous Solution Provider/Partner in the list. Click on their name or the **Details** link.
3. **Revoke Permissions:** In the details overlay, click the **Assign assets** button. Select the **WhatsApp accounts** asset type. Uncheck or use the toggles to remove all permissions granted to the previous provider concerning the WABA. Confirm the revocation to sever the final administrative ties, ensuring a completely clean slate for onboarding with Heyy.
